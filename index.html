Kings & Quadraphages

How to Play Kings & Quadraphages

Objective

Trap your opponent's King so it cannot move to any adjacent cell.

Game Setup

  • Each player has 1 King and 30 Quadraphages
  • Player 1 (Blue) starts at the top, Player 2 (Red) at the bottom

Turn Sequence

  1. Move your King: Click your King, then click an adjacent empty cell (any of the 8 surrounding cells)
  2. Place a Quadraphage: Click any empty cell to place a blocker

Rules

  • Kings can move one cell in any direction (like chess)
  • Kings cannot move onto Quadraphages or the other King
  • Quadraphages stay where placed for the entire game
  • You must complete both actions each turn

Winning

You win when your opponent's King has no valid moves at the start of their turn!
